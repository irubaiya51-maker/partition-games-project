How to Play

• The diagram is a left-justified stack of rows (a Young diagram).
• On your turn, choose to make either a row move or column move:
• Row Move: Pick any row whose length is at least the length of the row directly underneath. Remove one or more blocks from the right end, leaving at least one block behind.
• Column Move: Pick any column whose height is at least the height of the column directly to its right. Remove one or more blocks from the bottom, leaving at least one block behind.
• The player that removes the last remaining block anywhere on the board wins.
